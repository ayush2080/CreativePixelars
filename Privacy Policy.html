Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 05-01-2026

At Creative Pixelars, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you interact with us through our website or contact forms.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a form or contact us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Contact Details (Email Address and Mobile Number)
  • Any additional details you voluntarily provide (such as project requirements)
We only collect information that is necessary for communication and service-related purposes.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal information is collected and used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Communicate about graphic design services
  • Discuss project details such as timelines and pricing
We do not use your information for purposes unrelated to our services.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR-Aligned)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent, provided when you submit the form
  • Legitimate business interest, such as responding to inquiries
You may withdraw your consent at any time.

4. Communication Consent

By submitting your details, you consent to being contacted by Creative Pixelars via:

  • Email
  • Phone Call
  • SMS or messaging platforms (if applicable)
You may opt out of communications at any time by contacting us directly.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data may only be disclosed:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our legal rights or prevent fraud
  • When necessary to provide services, you request (with confidentiality safeguards)

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal data against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Alteration
  • Disclosure or destruction
While we strive to protect your data, no internet-based system can be guaranteed as completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfil the purpose of communication
  • Meet legal or regulatory requirements
Once no longer required, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
